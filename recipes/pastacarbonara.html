Pasta carbonara recipe

Pasta carbonara is a tasty and easy dish to make. Can be eaten as a lunch or dinner

Ingredients:

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 150g bacon
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 deciliters cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ deciliters grated parmesan
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Boil the spaghetti and drain the water
  2. Cut the bacon in pieces and mince the garlic
  3. Fry the bacon and garlic on pan
  4. Mix in a bowl: cream, eggs, parmesan and spices
  5. Mix the spaghetti and bacon together
  6. Add the cream mixture to the pasta, make sure the pasta is still hot
  7. Serve immediatly. Add grated parmesan and black pepper on top
