Scrambled eggs recipe

This is the recipe for the world's best scrambled eggs! Can be eaten as a breakfast or an afternoon snack

Ingredients for 2 portions:

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • A pinch of black pepper
  • Olive oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Break and whisk the eggs in a bowl
  2. Add milk and spices to the eggs
  3. Add the eggs on a hot pan
  4. Fry until ready
